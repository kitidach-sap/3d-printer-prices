# Project Vision & Strategy (`PROJECT.md`)

## Core Concept
**3D Printer Prices** (3d-printer-prices.com) is currently an affiliate-driven product catalog.
Our goal is to **transform it into a decision-tool platform**.

## Why the Pivot? (The "Moat")
Currently, the site aggregates price and spec information. While useful, it is easily replicable by competitors and doesn't provide a strong reason for users to return once they've made a purchase decision.

To build a strong "moat" (competitive defense) and improve SEO/retention, we must help users answer questions that Amazon cannot answer in one click:
- *Which printer fits my use case?*
- *What is my total setup cost?*
- *What accessories do I actually need?*

## Core Value Proposition
**Go from *"here are products"* to *"here is exactly what you should buy and why"*.**

## Key Strategies for Growth
1. **Interactive Tools:** Build calculators and quizzes. These convert browsing traffic into decision traffic.
2. **Contextual Comparisons:** Shift from generic specs to "Use-Case" matching (e.g., "Best for ABS", "Best for Miniatures").
3. **Long-Tail SEO via Guides & Tool Pages:** Target queries related to ownership cost, material compatibility, and model vs. model comparisons.

## Audience
- **Primary:** Beginners looking for their first 3D printer.
- **Secondary:** Hobbyists looking to upgrade or add specific capabilities (Resin, Multi-color, Enclosed).
