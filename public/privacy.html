Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how 3D Printer Prices ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at 3d-printer-prices.com. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our site. However, certain data may be collected automatically or through third-party services:

  • Usage data — Pages visited, time spent on pages, referral sources, browser type, device type, and operating system. This data is collected anonymously through analytics tools.
  • Cookies and local storage — Small data files stored on your device to remember preferences (such as dark/light theme) and support advertising and analytics.
  • IP address — Your IP address may be collected by third-party services (Google AdSense, analytics) for geolocation and fraud prevention. We do not store IP addresses directly.

We do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us via email.

How We Use Information

The data collected through our site is used for the following purposes:

  • Site functionality — Storing your theme preference (dark/light mode) and comparison selections via browser local storage.
  • Analytics — Understanding how visitors use our site so we can improve content, features, and user experience.
  • Advertising — Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdSense based on your interests and browsing activity.
  • Affiliate tracking — When you click a product link, Amazon Associates may use cookies to attribute qualifying purchases for commission purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdSense — Displays advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.
  • Amazon Associates — Product links on our site direct you to Amazon. Amazon may use cookies to track referrals. Purchases made through our links are subject to Amazon's Privacy Notice.
  • Google Fonts — We load the Inter font from Google Fonts. Google may collect usage data when fonts are loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Theme preference (dark/light mode), stored via browser localStorage. These do not expire and are never sent to a server.
  • Advertising cookies — Set by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads and limit ad frequency. These cookies may persist for up to 13 months.
  • Analytics cookies — Used to understand traffic patterns and page performance. These typically expire within 2 years.
  • Affiliate cookies — Set by Amazon when you click a product link, used to track referrals. Amazon cookies typically expire within 24 hours.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality and ad personalization. You can also use browser extensions like Google Analytics Opt-Out to prevent analytics tracking.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access — You can request information about what data (if any) we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion — You can request that we delete any personal data we may hold. Since we do not collect personal data directly, this primarily applies to data held by third-party services.
  • Right to opt out — You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings or by adjusting your browser's cookie preferences.
  • Do Not Sell My Personal Information (CCPA) — We do not sell personal information to third parties. Ad personalization through Google AdSense is based on Google's own data collection and can be opted out of as described above.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Data Retention

We do not maintain a user database or store personal information on our servers. Theme preferences are stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data. Third-party cookies are subject to the retention policies of their respective providers (Google, Amazon).

Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS to encrypt data in transit. We do not store user data on our servers, which minimizes security risk. Product data (prices, ratings) is sourced from publicly available Amazon listings and stored in our database for comparison purposes only.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make significant changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
