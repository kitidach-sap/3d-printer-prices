About 3D Printer Prices

We're a small team of 3D printing enthusiasts on a mission to make buying a 3D printer easier, cheaper, and more transparent.

🎯 Our Mission

Buying a 3D printer can be overwhelming. There are hundreds of models across different price ranges, technologies, and use cases. Marketing claims are everywhere, but honest, data-driven comparisons are hard to find.

We built 3D Printer Prices to solve that problem. Our goal is simple: help you find the right 3D printer at the best price — without the confusion, hype, or hidden agendas. Whether you're a complete beginner looking for your first FDM printer, a miniatures hobbyist shopping for a resin machine, or a seasoned maker hunting for a deal, we want to give you the data you need to make a confident decision.

👤 Who We Are

3D Printer Prices was founded in 2025 by a small team of makers, engineers, and data enthusiasts based in Southeast Asia. Our founder has been involved in 3D printing since the early days of consumer FDM, building and modifying printers, testing dozens of filaments, and helping friends choose their first machines.

After years of manually comparing specs and prices across different retailers, we decided to build the tool we wished existed — a daily-updated database that tracks real prices, ratings, and specifications across hundreds of products.

We're not a large company with a PR team. We're independent makers who care deeply about the 3D printing community and want to contribute something genuinely useful.

📊 How It Works

Every day, our automated pipeline collects and verifies data from Amazon product listings. Here's what makes our approach different:

  • Daily price updates — Our scraper runs every day so you always see current pricing, not stale data from weeks ago.
  • AI-powered classification — We use Google Gemini to clean up product names, identify brands, classify printer types, and flag junk listings. Strict anti-hallucination rules ensure the AI never invents specifications.
  • Multi-step verification — Each product goes through discovery, enrichment, detail-page scraping, and quality gating before appearing on the site.
  • Transparent scoring — Our ranking algorithm evaluates value, beginner-friendliness, speed, build volume, and user ratings. No paid placements. No sponsored rankings.

For a deep dive into our data pipeline and scoring methodology, visit our Methodology page.

💡 What We Offer

Beyond price comparison, we provide a growing set of free tools and resources for the 3D printing community:

  • Price Comparison — Browse and filter hundreds of 3D printers, filaments, and accessories with real-time Amazon pricing.
  • True Cost Calculator — Estimate the real 1-year cost of owning a 3D printer, including electricity, materials, and maintenance.
  • Material Compatibility Explorer — Find which filaments work with your printer and learn about different material properties.
  • Top Picks Rankings — Data-driven recommendations for specific use cases like beginners, budget buyers, speed enthusiasts, and resin detail.
  • Buying Guides & Blog — In-depth articles covering everything from beginner basics to advanced material selection.
