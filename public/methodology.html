📊 Our Methodology

Transparency is important to us. Here's exactly how we collect data, score products, and make recommendations — so you can make informed decisions with confidence.

Step 1: Automated Discovery

Our scraper searches Amazon daily for 3D printers, filaments, and accessories across multiple categories (FDM, Resin/SLA, PLA filament, tools & accessories, 3D pens).

Step 2: AI Enrichment

Each product is analyzed by AI (Google Gemini) to extract a clean product name, identify the brand, classify the printer type, and flag low-quality listings. This step has strict anti-hallucination rules — the AI cannot guess specifications not present in the title.

Step 3: Detail Verification

Individual product pages are scraped to verify the actual brand, extract high-resolution images, current pricing, star rating, review count, and technical specifications.

Step 4: Quality Gating

Products flagged as "junk titles" (e.g., accessory bundles listed as printers, unclear product names) are hidden from the catalogue until verified data is available.

💰 Pricing & Updates

All prices shown are in USD and sourced from Amazon.com. Key pricing details:

  • Update frequency: Prices are refreshed daily via our automated scraper
  • Discount calculation: We compare the current price against the listed original price to calculate discount percentages
  • Price history: Every price update is timestamped so we can show when data was last verified
  • Multiple sources: Prices from detail page scrapes always take priority over search result prices

⭐ How Scoring Works

Products are evaluated on multiple dimensions. Each score is derived from real product specifications, reviews, and pricing — never from paid placement.

🎯 Value Score
Price relative to features and category average. Lower price + higher specs = better value.
👶 Beginner Score
Based on ease of assembly, auto-leveling features, enclosed build chamber, and community support.
⚡ Speed Score
Derived from max print speed, acceleration capabilities, and direct drive vs bowden setup.
🔧 Maintenance Score
Estimated maintenance burden based on printer type, enclosure, and build complexity.
📦 Build Volume
Actual print area dimensions (L × W × H) extracted from product specifications.
⭐ User Rating
Amazon star rating and review count. Higher rating with more reviews = higher confidence.

🎯 How Recommendations Are Made

Our "Find My Printer" quiz uses AI to match your answers against our product database:

  • Budget: Your price range filters products within that range
  • Experience level: Beginners get printers with auto-leveling, easy setup, and good community support
  • Use case: Miniatures → resin printers; functional parts → FDM printers with high temp materials

Important: Recommendations are based entirely on product specifications and user requirements. We do not accept payment for placement.

🏷️ "Best For" Badges

Products earn badges based on objective, rule-based criteria — not editorial opinion:

  • 🏆 Best for Beginners: High beginner score, auto bed leveling, under $500, 4+ star rating
  • 💰 Budget Pick: Under $250 with 4+ stars and solid feature set
  • ⚡ High Speed: Advertised print speed ≥ 300mm/s or direct drive with high acceleration
  • 🎨 Resin Detail: Resin/SLA printer with ≤ 50µm layer resolution
  • 📦 Large Build Volume: Build volume exceeding 300mm in any dimension
  • 🔥 Good for ABS: Enclosed build chamber with heated bed ≥ 100°C

Source Priority

When multiple sources have data for the same field, we follow a strict priority chain:

  • Manual review (highest priority — never overwritten)
  • Amazon detail page (direct from product page)
  • Trusted seed data (verified reference data)
  • AI analysis (lowest priority — can be overwritten by any above)
